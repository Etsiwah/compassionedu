# Deploy CompassionEdu Frontend to Vercel - CMD Steps

## Step 1: Open Command Prompt
1. Press Windows + R
2. Type: cmd
3. Press Enter

## Step 2: Navigate to Frontend Folder
cd c:\Users\kwesi\Desktop\compassionedu-main\frontend

## Step 3: Deploy to Vercel Production
npx vercel --prod

## Step 4: Answer the prompts:
When prompted, answer:
- "Set up and deploy...?" → Press Enter (Yes)
- "Which scope?" → Select your account (Press Enter)
- "Link to existing project?" → Type: y (then press Enter)
- "What's the name of your existing project?" → Type: compassion-project (then press Enter)

The deployment will start and show progress. Wait for it to complete (2-3 minutes).

## Step 5: Get Your Production URL
When done, it will show:
✅ Production: https://your-url.vercel.app

Copy that URL and share it with your friend!

## Troubleshooting:
If you get an error about "Which team?", just press Enter to select your personal account.
